Lasagna Recipe

A nice lasagna for a nice autumn.

This is one of my favorite recipes for dinner as you will always have some left over for lunch the next day. If you have the time, I highly recommend trying it out. I promise that you won't regret it.

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

So, in order to make this recipe you will want to follow these steps carefully.

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
